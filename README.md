## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI. It provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional/store-level analysis.

The dashboard is designed with a **premium chocolate-themed UI** to deliver both business insights and strong visual storytelling.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Understand customer behavior (new vs repeat customers)
* Evaluate store and regional performance
* Track monthly and growth trends (MOM %)

---

## 📁 Dataset

The analysis is based on the following tables:

* **Sales**
* **Customers**
* **Products**
* **Stores**
* **Calendar**

---

## 📌 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Overview

* Total Revenue: **25.49M**
* Total Units Sold: **3M**
* Total Orders: **1M**
* Total Customers: **50K**
* Avg Order Value: **25.49**

✔ Highlights:

* Praline & White chocolate lead revenue
* Canada & UK are top-performing regions
* Stable monthly sales trend

---

### 2️⃣ Product Analysis

* Top Product: **Dark Chocolate 50%**
* Lowest Product: **Truffle Chocolate 60%**
* Category contribution analysis
* Top & Bottom product performance

---

### 3️⃣ Customer Analysis

* High repeat customer rate (~97%)
* Top customer identified
* Customer spending distribution
* City-wise and country-wise insights

---

### 4️⃣ Store Analysis

* Top Store: **Chocolate Store 75**
* Lowest Store: **Chocolate Store 4**
* City-level revenue insights
* Store performance comparison

---

### 5️⃣ Time Analysis

* Peak Month: **January**
* MOM growth shows slight fluctuations
* Monthly & quarterly trend analysis

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Strong customer retention indicates brand loyalty
* Balanced product contribution across categories
* Stable demand across months
* Consistent performance across regions
